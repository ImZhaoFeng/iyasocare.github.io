About IYASOCARE & Our Expertise

  1. Who is IYASOCARE MEDICAL? Are you a factory or just a trading company?

    We are Chongqing Iyasocare Medical Co., Ltd., a professional manufacturer and trading company integrated since our founding. This means we control the entire production process, ensuring consistent quality and direct-from-factory pricing. We are not just a trader; we are the source.

  2. What makes your team experienced in the wheelchair industry?

    While our company is built on fresh energy, our technical core team brings over 15 years of dedicated experience in the wheelchair industry. This expertise is backed by a strong R&D department of 8 Master’s and PhD-level engineers, and a team of 50 skilled technicians. We understand the nuances of mobility needs because we've been perfecting them for over a decade.

  3. What are your company's main strengths and certifications?

    Our strength lies in our commitment to certified quality and innovation. We operate a 10,000+ m² production facility with 4 dedicated production lines, capable of an annual output of up to 200,000 units. A new 10,000 m² smart factory is also under construction to meet future demand.

    We hold internationally recognized certifications including TUV, ISO 13485 (Medical devices), CE, and CIBG, ensuring our products meet rigorous global standards.

  4. Does IYASOCARE have its own patents and R&D capabilities?

    Absolutely. Innovation is at our core. Our R&D team, led by PhD and Master's degree holders, has already secured over 80 utility model and design patents. This allows us to continuously develop unique, functional, and comfortable mobility solutions for our global partners.

Our Products, Quality & Materials

  1. What types of products does IYASOCARE specialize in?

    Our main expertise lies in a wide range of wheelchairs, including Electric Wheelchairs, Manual Wheelchairs, and specialized Beach Wheelchairs. Our product portfolio also extends to stair-climbing wheelchairs, off-road wheelchairs, mobility scooters, rollators, crutches, medical beds, and commode chairs, offering comprehensive mobility and care solutions.

  2. What materials are used in your wheelchairs, and how do they affect quality?

    We select materials based on the chair's intended use and required durability:

    • For lightweight and portable models, we primarily use aviation-grade aluminum alloy.
    • For our heavy-duty off-road and outdoor wheelchairs requiring maximum stability, we use high-strength carbon steel.
    • For our premium, ultra-lightweight line, we utilize carbon fiber, with our lightest carbon fiber wheelchair weighing only 12 kg, ideal for frequent travelers.
  3. How can I be sure about the quality of IYASOCARE wheelchairs?

    Quality is assured through a multi-stage process. Before mass production, all new models undergo rigorous testing, including fatigue tests, impact tests, compression tests, and stability tests. During production, we conduct random spot checks and extreme testing. Finally, before packaging, every single wheelchair undergoes a comprehensive quality inspection using both AI-powered systems and skilled human inspectors to ensure only flawless products are shipped.

  4. Can you provide detailed specifications like exact weight for a specific model?

    We certainly can. Because we offer a wide variety of models with different configurations (e.g., frame material, wheel type, accessories), specific data like exact weight and dimensions varies by model. Once you identify a model you're interested in, please contact our sales team, and they will provide you with the detailed specification sheet immediately.
