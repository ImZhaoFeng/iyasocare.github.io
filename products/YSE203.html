Product Overview

The IYASOCARE YSE203 Foldable Electric Stair Climbing Wheelchair is engineered to solve mobility challenges in multi-story environments. As a B2B-focused manufacturer (OEM/ODM available), we prioritize durability, safety, and ease of use for global wholesalers, medical institutions, and caregivers. This model combines crawler-type stair climbing technology with a 24V dual-motor system (250W climbing motor + 250W×2 driving motors), enabling smooth navigation of stairs with slopes <45° and depths >1100mm.
