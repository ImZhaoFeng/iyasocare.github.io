YSE301 Electric Standing Wheelchair – Overview

Factory Direct Customizable Options Available CE / ISO 13485 Certified

The YSE301 electric standing wheelchair is a revolutionary mobility aid designed to improve the quality of life for individuals with limited mobility. Its primary function allows users to transition from a sitting to a standing position electrically, promoting better blood circulation, reducing pressure sores, and facilitating social interaction at eye level. This stand-up power wheelchair combines robust construction with advanced features for both rehabilitation and daily activities. With a 45km driving range and adjustable seat height from 120cm to 182cm, it offers unparalleled independence and versatility.

Key Benefits of the Standing Function

  • Health: Helps maintain bone density, improves circulation, and reduces muscle spasms.
  • Independence: Allows users to reach high shelves, interact face-to-face, and perform tasks from a standing position.
  • Comfort: Reduces pressure on the sitting area, alleviating discomfort during long periods.
