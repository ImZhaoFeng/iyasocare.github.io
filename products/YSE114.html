YSE114 Heavy Duty Electric Wheelchair – Overview

Factory Direct Customizable Options Available CE / ISO 13485 Certified

The YSE114 heavy duty electric wheelchair is engineered to provide reliable mobility for users requiring higher weight capacity. With a robust steel frame supporting up to 300kg (660lbs), it is one of the strongest bariatric electric wheelchairs in its class. Powered by dual 500W motors (1000W total), it effortlessly handles inclines and uneven terrain. The large 24V 55Ah lead-acid battery delivers an impressive 40-50km driving range, making it suitable for all-day use. Despite its strength, the chair features a foldable design for convenient transport and storage.
