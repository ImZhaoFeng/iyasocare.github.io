Product Overview

The IYASOCARE YSE204 is a cutting-edge electric stair climbing wheelchair engineered for B2B clients, including hospitals, hotels, schools, and residential care facilities. Combining portability, durability, and advanced stair-climbing technology, it addresses the critical need for safe emergency evacuation and multi-floor accessibility for mobility-impaired individuals.
