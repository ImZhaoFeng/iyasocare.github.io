Company Profile

Medical Equipment Manufacturer & Global Rehabilitation Solutions Provider

Chongqing Iyasocare Medical Co., Ltd. is a vertically integrated manufacturer and exporter specializing in medical devices R&D, production, and sales. With a core focus on rehabilitation products, we design and manufacture high-quality wheelchairs, walkers, rollators, and electric wheelchairs for global B2B partners.

Our mission is to deliver cost-effective, reliable, and innovative medical solutions that empower distributors, healthcare institutions, and retailers to meet growing market demands.

Factory Strength

Modern Production Base for Scalable B2B Orders

Established in 2019, our self-owned factory in Chongqing, China, features dedicated workshops for stamping, injection molding, welding, and final assembly. This integrated setup ensures:

  • Strict quality control across all production stages
  • Flexible production capacity to handle bulk orders
  • Competitive pricing through vertical integration

With years of industry expertise, we prioritize precision engineering and compliance with international standards (e.g., ISO 13485) to serve partners in Europe, North America, and Southeast Asia.
